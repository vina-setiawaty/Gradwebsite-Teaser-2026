Industrial Design
Graduation Showcase

We warmly invite you to join us at the DID Graduation Show 2026.

This year’s graduation showcase celebrates the design journeys behind the graduating cohort from the Division of Industrial Design, shaped by curiosity, rigor, and intent.

The works on display sit at the intersection of play and precision, imagination and purpose. This duality reflects how our students think, make and adapt. In an ever evolving world, we stay ready for what’s next.
